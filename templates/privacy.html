Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Spendly, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our personal expense tracking service, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it safe. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic log data such as pages visited and actions taken, used to diagnose errors and improve the app.

We do not collect payment details, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account information.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance.
  • To send essential service emails (e.g., password resets). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. We use industry-standard practices including password hashing and encrypted connections (HTTPS) to protect your information. While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely immune to security risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a small number of trusted third-party services (for example, a hosting provider or error-monitoring tool). These services access only the minimum data needed to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy policies. We do not embed third-party advertising or social-media tracking scripts.

6. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in between page loads. This cookie contains no personally identifiable information and is cleared when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking or analytics cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — you can view all expense data you have entered at any time.
  • Correction — you can edit or delete any individual record.
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data permanently.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app account settings or contact us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service evolves. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please get in touch via the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
